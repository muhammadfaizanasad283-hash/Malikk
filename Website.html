About GTTI Punjab

The Technical Education & Vocational Training Authority (GTTI) Punjab is a premier organization committed to providing high-quality technical education and vocational training. Our aim is to produce a skilled workforce that meets the demands of both local and international industries, contributing significantly to economic development and poverty alleviation.

GTTI offers a diverse range of market-driven programs in various trades and technologies. With a network of institutions across Punjab, we ensure accessibility and relevance of training. Our state-of-the-art workshops, experienced instructors, and industry linkages provide students with practical skills and career opportunities, fostering self-reliance and entrepreneurship.
